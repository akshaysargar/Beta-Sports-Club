Kaustubh Birje

Founder

Kaustubh Birje was a student of Vidya Bhavan School, MIT Jr. College & Garware College of Commerce (BBA Dept.) & actively participated in various extracurricular activities of School & College. MES GARWARE BBA Team won football Trophy & Kaustubh was one of the Team Member. He was one of the leading student members at front end for the preparation of challenging NACC Accreditation process.

He has been invited by M/S Kaveri Sports Club-PUNE at KARNATAKA SCHOOL - Kothrud for Part-time Football coaching to young students between ages 07 to 20 years. He has handled this activity with only 5students & now grown that club for more than 100 students.

He is a certified 'D' License Coach under ALL INDIA FOOTBALL FEDERATION.

December 2014 he initiated his own Football Club (BETA Sports Club) at MIT School, Kothrud. The club started with 3 students. And now, the club is successfully training more than 40 students within a year. Later, in 2015 he started with another center in Bavdhan. Now the club trains more than 100 students.
