Football
Football

A form of sport played between two teams of 11 players, in which the ball may be advanced by kicking or by bouncing it off any part of the body but the arms and hands, except in the case of the goalkeepers, who may use their hands to catch, carry, throw, or stop the ball.

Famous Players:Sunil Chetri(IND) , Lionel Messi(ARG) ,Cristiano Ronaldo(POR)

Basketball
Basketball

A game played by two teams of usually five players each on a rectangular court having a raised basket or goal at each end, points being scored by tossing the ball through the opponent's basket.

Famous Players : Lebron James(USA) , Stephen Curry(USA) , Pau Gasol(ESP)

Athletics
Athletics

Athletics is a collection of sporting events that involve competitive running, jumping, throwing, and walking. The most common types of athletics competitions are track and field, road running, cross country running, and race walking.

Famous Athletes : Usain Bolt(JAM) , Mo Farah(GBR)

Tennis
Tennis

A game played on a rectangular court by two players or two pairs of players equipped with rackets, in which a ball is driven back and forth over a low net that divides the court in half.

Famous Players: Sania Mirza(IND) , Roger Federer(SUI) , Rafael Nadal(ESP) , Novak Djokovic(SER)

Yoga
Yoga

Yoga is a group of physical, mental, and spiritual practices or disciplines which originated in ancient India.

Great Yogis : Maharishi Patanjali , Adi Shankaracharya , Swami Vivekananda

Swimming
Swimming

Swimming is an individual or team sport and activity. Competitive swimming is one of the most popular Olympic sports, with events in butterfly, backstroke, breaststroke, freestyle, and individual medley. In addition to these individual events, Olympic swimmers also participate in relays.

Famous Swimmers : Michael Phelps(USA) , Ian thorpe(AUS)

A game played on a rectangular court by two players or two pairs of players equipped with light rackets used to volley a shuttlecock over a high net that divides the court in half.

Famous Player : Saina Nehwal(IND) , Carolina Marin(ESP)
